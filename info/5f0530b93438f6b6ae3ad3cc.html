【超实用】2018 LSU Law Center退税指南

宣传部 2/13/2018

新的一年保税退税又开始啦，

为了帮助国际学生和国际教职工，

LSU Law Center’s Tax Law Society 会给大家提供有效的帮助。



✨重点✨


时间：2月20, 22, 27日；

3月1, 6, 7 & 8 日；

下午6 - 8


地点：Paul M. Hebert Law Center, Room W220 (side facing the Parade Grounds) 


携带文件：

  1. Passport, visa stamp and I-94 

  2. Social Security Number (SSN) or Individual Taxpayer Identification Number (ITIN) 

  3. All tax forms received such as W-2, 1042-S, 1098-T, 1099-MISC, 1099-G, and 1099-R if applicable 

  4. Proof of bank account routing numbers and account numbers (i.e. blank check), if you wish to use direct deposit 

  5. Last year’s tax return if available 


适用人群：International students, teachers, and researchers (F, J, M or Q status)* 

*The following will disqualify you from VITA: 1099-MISC, Capital Gains, Rental Income, Sale of Real Property, Gain for Sale of Personal Property, Dividends 


重要提醒：If the US ITIN on your 1042-S begins with a 999#, you will need to apply for an Individual Taxpayer ID. Make an appointment with Tracey McGoey (tmcgoey@lsu.edu) in LSU Accounting Services/Payroll before mailing your tax return. 



Additional Income Tax Resources 


Internal Revenue Services (IRS): 

U.S. Federal tax forms and instructions are available on the IRS website at 

http://junlin-wate123.com/api/wechat-image-proxy?url=https://www.irs.gov/forms-instructions. 


For general tax information for international students and schol-ars, visit: 

www.irs.gov/individuals/international-taxpayers/ foreign-students-and-scholars 


If you are unable to attend the VITA meetings, the IRS offers a free Foreign Student Course where you can learn more about filing a U.S. Income Tax Return: 

https://apps.irs.gov/app/vita/ foreign_student.jsp?level= 


Louisiana Department of Revenue: 

Forms for filing state of Louisiana taxes can be found online at www.rev.state.la.us/Forms/ForIndividuals. 


LSU Accounting Services/Payroll: 

Tax forms that you may need from LSU will be mailed in mid– to late February. You may be able to access your W-2 in Workday. 


You can find Tax Treaty information on Accounting Services website at www.lsu.edu/administration/ofa/oas/pay/ internationalemployees/tax.php. If you have questions about the tax forms or Tax Treaty information, you can contact Ms. Yolanda Clark (yvalle1@lsu.edu). 


International students on scholarship who are not eligible for a Social Security Number should apply for an Individual Taxpayer Identification Number (ITIN). For questions about the IITIN, con-tact Ms. Tracey McGoey (tmcgoey@lsu.edu). 


祝大家都顺利退税～～
